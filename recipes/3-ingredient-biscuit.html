3 Ingredient Buiscuit

Description

This tasty recipie was passed down from my mother to me, It has always been a family favourite for those sweet tooth moments. Not only is it a yummy quick treat it also only takes 3 Ingredients which you are most likely to have laying around in your pantry!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 500 g rama(margarine)
  • 1 cup icing sugar

Steps

  1. Melt the margarine into liquid then in a bowl combine the flour and sugar with the margarine liquid, mix well until dough is formed and combined well.
  2. use the dough cutters to cut any shape of choice then bake using 180Â° for about 15 minutes to 20 minutes remove from the oven serve and enjoy!
